CodeStar --> service for creating, managing, and working with AWS projects
  CodeStar well integrates with CodeDeploy, CodePipeline, CodeCommit, codeBuild, CloudWatch
  Benefits --> 
    can create templates for projects and reuse them
    integrate some IDE with CodeStar
    Helps manage users and access they require to interact with AWS services (saves you from create and manage IAM policies, as everything can be written in templates)
    visualize and collaborate on your projects at one same place
        like, application activity metrics - CPU utilization,  JIRA issue tracking, commit history, continous deployment
